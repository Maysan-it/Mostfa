Regulatory & Market Context

KSA e-invoicing (Fatoorah) is in two phases: Phase 1 (Generation) effective Dec 4, 2021 for all VAT taxpayers (excl. non-residents), and Phase 2 (Integration) rolling in waves since Jan 1, 2023, with at least 6 months‚Äô notice per wave [ZATCA](https://zatca.gov.sa/en/E-Invoicing/Introduction/Pages/Roll-out-phases.aspx).

On June 27, 2025, ZATCA announced the 23rd wave of Phase 2: resident taxpayers with taxable turnover > SAR 750,000 in 2022/2023/2024 must comply between Jan 1‚ÄìMar 31, 2026 [EY](https://www.ey.com/en_gl/technical/tax-alerts/saudi-arabia-announces-23rd-wave-of-phase-2-e-invoicing-integration).

KSA VAT implementing changes (Apr 18, 2025) tightened VAT grouping, clarified marketplace rules, nominal supply adjustments, and added non-deductible input VAT categories; certain changes apply from 2026 [KPMG](https://kpmg.com/us/en/taxnewsflash/news/2025/04/saudi-arabia-vat-amendments-approved-effective.html).

UAE: Small Business Relief lets eligible resident businesses with revenue ‚â§ AED 3,000,000 elect to be treated as having no taxable income (cannot be QFZP or part of an MNE group) through Dec 31, 2026 [FTA](https://tax.gov.ae/en/taxes/corporate.tax/corporate.tax.topics/small.business.relief.23.aspx). VAT regulations also saw substantial clarifications via Public Clarification No. 40 (effective Nov 15, 2024) [KPMG](https://kpmg.com/us/en/taxnewsflash/news/2025/03/tnf-uae-guidance-on-recent-amendment-to-vat-regulation.html).

GCC highlights: Bahrain VAT standard rate is 10% since Jan 1, 2022 [Bahrain Govt](https://www.bahrain.bh/wps/portal/en/BNP/HomeNationalPortal/ContentDetailsPage/!ut/p/z0/lY9BT8JAEIX_ynpoogezQ6uAR5GEaoKgJgh7IdN2LCPtbOluG36-LfFA5ORxJu99-Z42eq2NYMs5eraCRXdvzHA7eL6HePwEsJzehfC2engfTuYDmM8i_UGiX7Q5D8WvyykMF_F4FI4m0WIV9RT-PhzMozapFU9Hr9eJVFuSADCxjVd-R2rPkme2DCDBXY0sKoQIAqipQE-Z8rbi1AWQN5x1SVXVLClXBTllv04A6uC25FS17Dr9U_-v3OWC_8o1joWcUyiZYmnJ-ZLEX3q2WDSkMMv6Hx7VdYv-pmuc43rz37W62s8-b83m6gcBLJvw/). Qatar has not introduced VAT yet but is expected to under the GCC framework [PwC](https://taxsummaries.pwc.com/qatar/corporate/other-taxes).
